Ever wanted to bring your Combine soldier friend along for a road trip? Or maybe evacuate Citizens in your LVS helicopter? Now you can!

This addon allows NPCs to enter vehicles as passengers, complete with [b]lifelike animations[/b], [b]dynamic head tracking[/b], [b]emotional reactions[/b], and [b]multi-passenger support[/b].

[h2]🎮 Quick Start[/h2]
[b]Easy Mode (Auto-Join):[/b] Press F7 → Enable Auto-Join → Get in vehicle
[b]Manual Mode:[/b] Hold C → Right-click NPC → Make Passenger → Enter vehicle
[b]Remove:[/b] Right-click passenger or hold R near dead bodies

[hr][/hr]

[h2]⚙️ Settings[/h2]
Press [b]F7[/b] or [b]Spawnmenu ➜ Utilities ➜ NPC Passengers[/b]

[hr][/hr]

[h2]🔧 Common Issues[/h2]
[b]NPCs floating:[/b] Adjust Position offsets (F7)
[b]Passengers dying in crashes:[/b] Normal - drive carefully!
[b]Can't remove dead bodies:[/b] Get close and hold R
[b]Auto-join not working:[/b] Check Auto-Join tab settings
