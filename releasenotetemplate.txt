## v2.4.1

### Bug Fixes
- **Fixed NPC head frozen after ejection** — all head/eye pose parameters are now reset to neutral when an NPC leaves the vehicle
- **Fixed NPC head jitter** — replaced `FrameTime()` delta with actual elapsed time in SmoothDamp, and corrected inverted pitch sign in eye target

### Improvements
- **Smoother head movement** — added 60 deg/s angular speed cap to prevent creepy fast snapping; hold times increased so NPCs rest on targets longer
- **Natural eye tracking** — eyes now stay within ±10° of the head direction instead of darting independently to random world positions
- **Idle head drift** — subtle ±2.5° micro-movement replaces the old sin/cos sway that fought SmoothDamp
- **Breathing applied to output only** — no longer causes the interpolation target to wiggle
- **Smooth drowsiness** — head drifts down gradually when falling asleep instead of snapping
- **Enemy focus** — head snaps 60% toward a newly spotted enemy immediately, with faster tracking while on a threat
